About

I'am Pranav

I’m a data scientist aiming for an analytics role, with experience as a Product Data Analyst at Assurant and a Marketing Data Analyst at Axis Bank. Skilled in SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and data storytelling, I excel at documentation, communication, and stakeholder management. My biggest impact has been prioritizing strategic enhancements to digital checking accounts at Axis Bank, such as optimizing onboarding strategies for customer acquisition, designing cross-selling frameworks through statistical analysis, and developing scalable data marts and dashboards. These efforts boosted customer acquisitions by over 60% and increased revenue per customer by more than 30%, drawn from a dataset of 20M users.

  • - Relationships are everything
